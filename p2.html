Ingredients:

  • 1 Whole Wheat burger bun
  • 1 medium boiled potato,mashed
  • 1/4 cup grated carrot
  • 1/4 cup finely chopped onions
  • 1 green chilli, finely chopped(optional)
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1/2 tsp garam masala
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 lettuce leaf
  • 2 tomato slices
  • mint yogurt sauce(optional)

Instructions:

  1. Prepare the burger patty:
    • In a bowl mix mashed potato, grated carrot, onions, green chilli, cumin powder, garam masala, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, and salt.
    • Add chiclpea flour(besan) to bind the mixture.
    • shape the mixture into a round patty.
    • Heat 1 tsp olive oil in a pan and cook the patty until brown on both sides.
  2. Assemble the Burger:
    • Slice the Whole Wheat Burger bun into halves.
    • Lightly toast the bun on a pan.
    • Spread mint yogurt sauce or green chutney on the lower half of the bun.
    • Place lettuce leaf, then add the crispy patty on the top.
    • Add tomato and cucumber slices
    • cover with the upper half of the bun
  3. Serve the healthy Indian-style burger with a side of yogurt dip or chutney.Enjoy!
