Ingredients:

For the Sundae:

  • 2 scoops butterscotch ice cream
  • 2 scoops chocolate ice cream
  • ¼ cup butterscotch sauce
  • ¼ cup chocolate syrup
  • 2 tablespoons crushed nuts (e.g., pecans or almonds)
  • 1 tablespoon mini chocolate chips
  • Whipped cream
  • 1 maraschino cherry
  • Chocolate shavings (for garnish)
  • 1 waffle cone (optional, for garnish)

For Homemade Butterscotch Ice Cream:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ¾ cup brown sugar
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ¼ cup crushed caramelized nuts (optional)

For Homemade Chocolate Ice Cream:

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • ¾ cup granulated sugar
  • ½ cup cocoa powder
  • ½ cup chocolate chips (melted)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

For Homemade Whipped Cream:

  • 1 cup heavy whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • ½ teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions:

To Make Butterscotch Ice Cream:

  1. In a saucepan, melt butter over medium heat and add brown sugar.
  2. Cook until sugar dissolves and turns into a smooth caramel.
  3. Slowly add milk while stirring, then add heavy cream, salt, and vanilla.
  4. Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for 2 hours.
  5. Churn in an ice cream maker according to manufacturer instructions.
  6. Mix in caramelized nuts (if using) and freeze for at least 4 hours.

To Make Chocolate Ice Cream:

  1. In a saucepan, heat milk, sugar, and cocoa powder over medium heat.
  2. Add melted chocolate chips and mix until smooth.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in heavy cream, vanilla, and salt.
  4. Chill the mixture for 2 hours.
  5. Churn in an ice cream maker and freeze for at least 4 hours.

To Make Whipped Cream:

  1. In a chilled mixing bowl, beat heavy cream until soft peaks form.
  2. Add powdered sugar and vanilla extract.
  3. Beat until stiff peaks form.
  4. Keep refrigerated until ready to use.

To Assemble the Sundae:

  1. Place 1 scoop of butterscotch ice cream in a serving glass or bowl.
  2. Add 1 scoop of chocolate ice cream next to it.
  3. Drizzle butterscotch sauce over the butterscotch ice cream and chocolate syrup over the chocolate ice cream.
  4. Sprinkle crushed nuts and mini chocolate chips on top.
  5. Add a generous dollop of whipped cream.
  6. Garnish with chocolate shavings, a cherry, and a waffle cone.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!

Tips:

Use homemade butterscotch sauce for an even richer flavor.

Chill serving glasses for a refreshing, cold dessert experience.
