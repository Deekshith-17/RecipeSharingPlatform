Molten Lava Cake

    Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup(115g) unsalted butter.
  • 6 oz(170g) semi-sweet choclate(chopped).
  • 2 large eggs.
  • 2 large egg yolks.
  • 1/4 cup(50g) granulated sugar.
  • 1/4 tsp salt.
  • 1/4 cup(30g) all-purpose flour.

    Instructions:

  1. preheat and prep:
    • Preheat oven to 425oF (220oC). Grease 4 ramekins with butter and dust them lightly with cocoa powder or flour.
  2. Melt Chocolate and Butter:
    • In a microwave-safe bowl, melt the butter and chocolate together in 20-second intervals, srirring each time, until smooth.
  3. Mix Wet Ingredients:
    • In another bowl, whisk eggs, egg yolks, sugar, and salt until slightly thickened and pale.
  4. Bake:
    • Pour the batter into the ramekins and place them on a baking sheet. Bake for 12-14 minutes. The edges should be set, but the center should be soft.
  5. Serve:
    • Let them rest for 1 minute, then run a knief around the edges and invert onto a plate. Serve immediately with icecream or berries.
