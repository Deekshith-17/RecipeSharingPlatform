Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup honey
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 cup warm milk
  • ½ cup chopped nuts (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and grease a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, mix flour, baking soda, cinnamon, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, whisk honey, oil, eggs, brown sugar, and vanilla extract.
  4. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet mixture, alternating with warm milk.
  5. Mix until smooth, then fold in nuts if using.
  6. Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan.
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  8. Let the cake cool completely before serving.

Tips:

For extra flavor, drizzle honey over the cake before serving.

Store the cake in an airtight container to keep it moist.
