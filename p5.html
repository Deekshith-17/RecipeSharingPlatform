Ingredients:

  • 1 pre-made pizza crust
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/4 cup sliced olives
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano

Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place the pizza crust on a baking sheet.
  3. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the crust.
  4. Sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  5. Add pepperoni, olives, and bell peppers.
  6. Sprinkle dried oregano for extra flavor.
  7. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the cheese melts and turns golden.
  8. Remove from oven, let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and serve.

Tips:

For a crispy crust, pre-bake the crust for 5 minutes before adding toppings.

Experiment with different toppings like mushrooms, onions, or extra cheese.
