  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Instructions:

  1. Blend strawberries and sugar in a blender until smooth.
  2. In a bowl, mix the strawberry puree with heavy cream, milk, vanilla extract, and salt.

Strawberry Ice Cream Sundae Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 scoops of homemade strawberry ice cream
  • 1/2 cup fresh strawberries (sliced)
  • 2 tablespoons strawberry sauce
  • Whipped cream
  • Chopped nuts (optional)
  • 1 wafer or waffle cone piece
  • 1 cherry (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Place two scoops of strawberry ice cream in a serving glass or bowl.
  2. Drizzle strawberry sauce over the ice cream.
  3. Top with fresh sliced strawberries and whipped cream.
  4. Sprinkle chopped nuts if desired.
  5. Insert a wafer and place a cherry on top.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy this refreshing summer treat!

Tip: You can drizzle melted chocolate or caramel sauce for extra flavor!
