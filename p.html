Classic Tomato Garlic Pasta

A simple yet delicious pasta recipe with fresh tomatoes and garlic.

Ingredients:

  • 200g pasta (spaghetti or penne)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Fresh basil leaves (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water according to the package instructions.
  2. In a pan, heat olive oil and saut√© the minced garlic until golden.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes and cook for 5-7 minutes.
  4. Season with salt, black pepper, and Italian seasoning.
  5. Drain the pasta and mix it with the sauce.
  6. Top with Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves.
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
